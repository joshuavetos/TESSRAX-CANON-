# PROTO-002: Notice of Epistemic Fragility (NEF-01)

## Purpose
Force an institution to acknowledge whether it can reconstruct truth
without its systems.

This is not an accusation.
It is a diagnostic boundary.

## The Notice (Template)

> We are requesting confirmation of the institution’s Epistemic Recovery Capability.
>
> Specifically:
> Can a human team, using only offline records, reconstruct the authoritative
> state of the following system within 24 hours?

Target System: ______________________  
Date of Test: ________________________

## Required Response (Binary)

- [ ] YES — Reconstruction is possible
- [ ] NO — Reconstruction is not possible

Narratives, explanations, or deferrals are invalid responses.

## Consequences

If YES:
- Institution affirms human sovereignty over truth

If NO:
- Institution admits epistemic dependency
- A DIAG-001 (ERT) score of ≤3 is implied
- The system is classified as Pathologically Entangled

## Legal Significance
This notice establishes:
- Knowledge of fragility
- Date of awareness
- Responsibility for continued operation
