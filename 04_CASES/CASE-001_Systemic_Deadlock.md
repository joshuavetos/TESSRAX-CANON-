# Case 001 — Systemic Deadlock

## Summary
A recurring failure mode where safety mechanisms become load-bearing infrastructure.

## Key Signals
- Halt conditions described as “unthinkable”
- Experts argue against intervention
- Continuity framed as moral obligation
- Reform attempts increase fragility

## Outcome
Governance collapses into narrative maintenance.
System persists without control.

## Lesson
A system that cannot survive pause has already failed.
