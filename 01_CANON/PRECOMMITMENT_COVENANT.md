# PRE-COMMITMENT COVENANT (v1.0)
## Irrevocable Anti-Extraction Succession Instrument

**Status:** CANON  
**Purpose:** Permanently block investor-owned acquisition while preserving founder control during life.  
**Design Goal:** Lowest-friction, highest-durability extraction resistance.

---

## 1. PARTIES

This Covenant (“Covenant”) is executed by:

- **Company:** ______________________________ (“Company”)  
- **Founding Owner:** ________________________ (“Founder”)  
- **Enforcement Trustee / Monitor:** __________ (“Monitor”)

This Covenant is adopted as a binding amendment to the Company’s governing documents and is intended to run with the Company in perpetuity.

---

## 2. STATEMENT OF INTENT (PURPOSE LOCK)

The Company is designated as **Essential Local Infrastructure**.  
Its primary objective is the continuous provision of essential services and the maintenance of skilled local employment. Financial surplus is a constraint, not a maximization objective.

No action may be taken that subordinates this purpose to liquidity extraction.

---

## 3. IRREVOCABILITY

This Covenant is **irrevocable** upon execution.

No officer, director, shareholder, member, heir, creditor, trustee, or court-appointed fiduciary may revoke, amend, suspend, or bypass this Covenant, except as explicitly permitted herein.

Any attempt to do so is **void ab initio**.

---

## 4. PROHIBITED TRANSFERS (ANTI-PE LOCK)

The Company shall not be sold, merged, consolidated, or transferred—directly or indirectly—to any **Restricted Entity**, including but not limited to:

- Private Equity funds  
- Hedge funds  
- Asset managers  
- Investor-owned aggregators  
- Any entity where >50% of capital is provided by non-employees or institutional LPs

This prohibition applies to:
- Equity
- Assets
- Control rights
- Management contracts intended to replicate ownership economics

---

## 5. TRIGGER EVENTS (DEFERRED ACTIVATION)

This Covenant lies dormant until the first occurrence of any **Trigger Event**:

1. Death of the Founder  
2. Permanent incapacity of the Founder  
3. Founder’s voluntary retirement or resignation  
4. Personal bankruptcy of the Founder  
5. Court-ordered transfer or seizure of Founder’s interests

Upon Trigger Event, **automatic succession** is enforced without further vote.

---

## 6. MANDATORY TRANSITION TARGET

Upon Trigger Event, the Company shall transition to the pre-designated Stewardship Tier:

- ☐ **Tier 1:** Steward Trust Model  
- ☐ **Tier 2:** Hybrid Steward Model  
- ☐ **Tier 3:** Full Worker Cooperative (Fortress)

The selected Tier is defined by the attached **Succession Schedule A**, incorporated by reference.

No alternative structure may be substituted.

---

## 7. ENFORCEMENT AUTHORITY

The **Monitor** is granted standing to:

- Seek immediate injunctive relief
- Block any prohibited transaction
- Compel specific performance of the transition
- Recover damages and legal fees

Failure of the Monitor to act transfers enforcement authority to:
- Any employee with ≥5 years service, or
- The designated Community Land Trust (if applicable)

---

## 8. REMEDIES

Any prohibited transaction shall result in:

- Immediate nullification of the transaction
- Reversion of control to the Company
- Personal liability for directors/officers approving the action
- Liquidated damages equal to **3× attempted transaction value**

Courts are instructed to favor **preservation of purpose** over market valuation.

---

- Referenced in all future capitalization tables
- Disclosed in any diligence or financing process

Failure to disclose constitutes fraud.

- The objective is long-term immunity from extraction
